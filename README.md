This Power BI dashboard was built using internal Ad Operations request-tracker data to monitor team workload, request distribution, placement volume, QA activities, and client-wise delivery trends. 
The solution provides end-to-end visibility into trafficking and QA operations, helping managers identify workload patterns, resource allocation, and process efficiency.

Project Type: Ad Operations Reporting Dashboard
Domain: Digital Advertising Operations (AdOps)

Business Problem

Ad Operations teams handle a large volume of requests related to campaign trafficking, reporting, QA reviews, meetings, troubleshooting, and client support. Tracking these requests through spreadsheets often makes it difficult to:

Monitor overall workload across team members.
Identify peak request periods.
Measure individual trafficker and QA performance.
Analyze client-wise demand.
Understand operational bottlenecks.
Generate management-ready performance reports.

Objective: Monitor, analyze, and optimize request management, trafficking workload, and QA performance through interactive business intelligence reporting.

Key Features

Request Volume Analysis

Tracks total requests by weekday.
Identifies peak workload periods and demand trends.



Trafficker Performance Monitoring

Monthly placement counts by trafficker.
Individual contribution and workload distribution.



QA Performance Tracking

QA completion analysis by QA personnel.
Distribution of QA activities across team members.
Client-wise QA request segmentation.



Client Analytics

Request and placement volume by client.
Identification of high-demand accounts requiring greater operational support.



Interactive Filtering

Reporting period slicers.
Request type filters (Campaign Trafficking, Reporting, Teams Chat/Call, Trafficking Sheet).
Dynamic drill-down capability for detailed analysis.



Key Insights from Dashboard

Wednesday recorded the highest request volume (145 requests), indicating the busiest operational day.
Clients such as Klaviyo and Wella generated the largest share of requests and placements.
Placement workload varied significantly across months, with March and June showing the highest activity levels.
QA activities were distributed across multiple team members, providing visibility into review ownership and capacity.

Business Value

Improved workload visibility across trafficking and QA teams.
Enabled data-driven resource planning and capacity management.
Reduced manual effort in monitoring operational performance.
Provided actionable insights for process optimization and team utilization.
